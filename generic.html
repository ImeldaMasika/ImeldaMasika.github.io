About Me

I am a dedicated data enthusiast who embarked on an exciting journey in Business Information Technology (BBIT) at Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology. Seeking to deepen my understanding of data science, I pursued further education at Moringa School, where I completed a Nano Degree program.

During my time at Moringa School, I immersed myself in the world of data science, mastering essential tools such as Python, SQL, Power BI, Data Scrapping, Machine Learning and Tableau. These skills have empowered me to extract meaningful insights from complex datasets, conduct sophisticated analyses, and craft engaging visualizations that tell compelling stories.

My academic background and hands-on experience have instilled in me a strong analytical mindset and a fervent passion for using data-driven approaches to solve real-world problems. I am eager to apply my expertise to projects that drive innovation and create positive impacts in various domains. With a thirst for knowledge and a commitment to continuous growth, I am enthusiastic about exploring new opportunities and making meaningful contributions in the dynamic field of data science and beyond. Let's connect and embark on a journey of innovation together!
